# **English Typing Course (Beginner to Advanced)**

## **Course Objective**

To help learners achieve **speed, accuracy, and confidence** in English typing using both **theoretical and practical** techniques.

---

## **Course Duration:** 4 Weeks (Can extend to 6 for slower learners)

---

## **Course Structure**

### ** WEEK 1: Introduction & Finger Placement**

#### **Lesson 1: Basics of Typing**

* What is typing & its importance
* Difference between Touch Typing and Hunt & Peck
* Understanding QWERTY Keyboard Layout

#### **Lesson 2: Home Row Keys**

* Finger placement on A–S–D–F & J–K–L–;
* Practice drills:

  ```
  asdf jkl; asdf jkl;
  aj aj aj; kj kj kj;
  ```

#### **Lesson 3: Posture & Hand Position**

* Correct sitting posture
* Wrist & finger movement
* Common mistakes to avoid

#### **Practice Goal:**

- 15 minutes daily practice
- Achieve **15–20 WPM** (words per minute)

---

### ** WEEK 2: Mastering Top & Bottom Rows**

#### **Lesson 4: Top Row Keys**

* Keys: Q–W–E–R–T–Y–U–I–O–P
* Drills:

  ```
  qwe rty uio p  
  weq reu yop t
  ```

#### **Lesson 5: Bottom Row Keys**

* Keys: Z–X–C–V–B–N–M
* Drills:

  ```
  zxcvbnm zxcvbnm  
  mnv cxb z
  ```

#### **Lesson 6: Combining All Rows**

* Practice full-sentence typing
* Example:

  ```
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
  ```

#### **Practice Goal:**

- 20–25 WPM
- Accuracy above **85%**

---

### ** WEEK 3: Numbers, Symbols, and Punctuation**

#### **Lesson 7: Number Row**

* 1 to 0 keys practice
* Use in sentences and data entry

#### **Lesson 8: Punctuation Marks**

* . , ? ! : ; “ ” ( )
* Correct spacing rules after punctuation

#### **Lesson 9: Shift Key & Capital Letters**

* Practice with capitalized words:

  ```
  My Name Is Navjot.
  ```

#### **Practice Goal:**

- 30–35 WPM
- Accuracy above **90%**

---

### ** WEEK 4: Advanced Practice & Speed Building**

#### **Lesson 10: Word & Sentence Drills**

* Common English words practice
* Daily use sentences typing

#### **Lesson 11: Paragraph Typing**

* Typing stories, news, or letters

#### **Lesson 12: Accuracy & Speed Test**

* Online typing test (e.g., typingtest.com or 10fastfingers.com)
* Evaluate progress

#### **Practice Goal:**

- 40–50+ WPM
- Accuracy above **95%**

---

## **Bonus Module: Professional Typing**

#### **Lesson 13: Formatting & Typing in MS Word**

* Alignment, spacing, font adjustments
* Typing professional documents

#### **Lesson 14: Data Entry & Form Filling**

* Typing structured text in forms/spreadsheets

#### **Lesson 15: Tips to Maintain Speed**

* Daily warm-up drills
* Consistency tracker
* Avoiding fatigue and errors

---

## **Final Certification Test**

* 5-minute typing test
* Evaluation on:

  * Speed (WPM)
  * Accuracy (%)
  * Formatting & presentation

---

## **Expected Outcome**

By the end of the course, you’ll:

* Type **40–60 words per minute** with 95%+ accuracy
* Type **without looking at the keyboard**
* Gain confidence for **data entry, exams, and office typing work**
