# 4-Week Typing Improvement Plan

### Current Goal:

* Increase speed by **15–30 WPM**
* Improve accuracy to **95%+**

---

## Daily Warm-Up (5 minutes) – EVERY DAY

Do this before starting your practice:

Type each line **5 times** (don’t look at keyboard):

```
asdf jkl;
fads jaks
asdfg hjkl;
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
```

Then:

```
12345 67890 
!@#$% ^&*()
```

---

## WEEK 1 – Finger position & accuracy

**Goal:** Learn correct finger placement

### Daily practice – 15 minutes

1. Go to: **typing.com** or **keybr.com**
2. Practice **Home Row & Top Row**
3. Focus:
   ✅ Accuracy
   ✅ Don’t look at keyboard
   ❌ Don’t rush

### Target by end of Week 1:

* **Speed:** 20–25 WPM
* **Accuracy:** 90%+

---

## WEEK 2 – Build consistency

**Goal:** Make fingers move automatically

### Daily practice – 20 minutes

1. 10 minutes on **typing.com – lessons**
2. 10 minutes on **10fastfingers.com – 1-minute test**
3. Practice this paragraph daily:

```
Learning to type is an important skill. 
It helps students and professionals work faster 
and more efficiently on computers.
```

### Target by end of Week 2:

* **Speed:** 30–35 WPM
* **Accuracy:** 92%+

---

## WEEK 3 – Add speed

**Goal:** Increase WPM

### Daily practice – 20–25 minutes

1. 10 minutes on **monkeytype.com**
2. 5 minutes: numbers + special characters
3. 10 minutes: typing paragraphs (no mistakes)

Important rule:

> If accuracy drops below 90%, SLOW DOWN.

### Target by end of Week 3:

* **Speed:** 40–45 WPM
* **Accuracy:** 95%+

---

## WEEK 4 – Real typing + mastery

**Goal:** Become fast & confident

### Daily practice – 25 minutes

1. Practice typing **real content**:

   * Emails
   * Blog paragraphs
   * Website content

2. Do **3 speed tests** daily

   * Try to beat your own record

### Target by end of Week 4:

* **Speed:** 50–60+ WPM
* **Accuracy:** 95–98%

---

## Weekly Test (VERY IMPORTANT)

Every Sunday do 3 tests on:

* **10fastfingers.com**
* **monkeytype.com**

---

##  Extra Tips (These give fast results)

✅ Do NOT look at keyboard
✅ Keep fingers on home row
✅ Sit straight
✅ Use all 10 fingers
✅ Practice every day (no break days)
